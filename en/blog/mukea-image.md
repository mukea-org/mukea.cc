# Mukea｜Standard Character Look

- Name: Mukea
- Gender: Female
- Visual age: 16
- Keywords: cute, approachable, energetic, fresh

## Body

- Height / Weight: 158 cm / 46 kg
- Proportion: 7.5-head standing ratio (light, energetic feel)

## Color Spec

- Main color: Sky blue `#B1DBEB`
- Main skin tone: `#FFF0E4` (fair)

## Face & Eyes

- Face shape: soft, youthful, slightly chubby short round doll-like face
- Iris: light blue `#BFD9E9` (radial gradient, darker inside / lighter outside), thin rim `#1B2A3A`
- Pupil: deep sea blue dot pupil `#234B7B`
- Accents: subtle radial highlight `#A9D6F2`; double catchlights `#FFFFFF`
- Eyelashes: black
- Eyebrows: willow-leaf brows `#89B2CD`

## Hair

- Hair color: based on the main color palette
- Length: about 3–4 cm below jawline, about 2–3 cm above shoulders; slightly inward-curving ends
- Bangs / side hair: light airy bangs; soft side strands that follow the face contour

## Ears (Cat Ears)

- Shape: upright triangular cat ears
- Outer: matches hair gradient
- Inner: soft pink `#FFC1D6`, with a small amount of white fine fluff `#FFFFFF`

## Outfit (Default)

- Top: pure white hoodie
- Bottom: cool gray-blue `#B9C3CD` cuffed cropped joggers, showing ~1 cm of ankle
- Shoes: white sneakers
