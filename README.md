# Mukea · 官网仓库

欢迎来到 Mukea 的官网仓库。这里是一个公开的“会一直在场”的据点：你可以在 **Issues** 里和我们讨论想法、反馈体验、分享使用场景；也可以在 **Releases** 里下载最新版本，直接把 Mukea 带回你的桌面。

Mukea 的设计不是“你命令、我执行”，而是更像一种有深度的陪伴：它在屏幕上有可见的形象，却不会喧哗打扰；它安静地理解你当下在做什么——浏览、观影、写作、设计、敲代码——并用背景式的存在感，帮你把孤独感放轻一点。

它也会更主动一些：当它察觉你卡住了、绕远路了、或是遇到瓶颈，会在合适的时刻递上一点线索、一句建议、一个更顺的做法。更重要的是，Mukea 会认真看见你的成果：不仅修修补补，更会理解你为什么做得好，并把那份“我懂你”的反馈送给你。

如果你愿意，让我们从一个 Issue 开始聊天吧。Mukea 在这儿，随时。

# Mukea · Official Repository

Welcome to Mukea’s official home on GitHub. This repository is **public**: feel free to start conversations in **Issues**, share your use cases, report rough edges, or brainstorm new ideas. When you’re ready to try it, head to **Releases** to download the latest build and bring Mukea to your desktop.

Mukea isn’t a “you command, I execute” tool. It’s a companion that stays present—visible on your screen, but never loud. It quietly understands what you’re doing in the digital world: browsing, watching, writing, designing, coding. That gentle background presence is there to make the screen feel a little less lonely.

And Mukea is proactive in a friendly way. Instead of waiting for you to ask the perfect question, it tries to notice when you’re stuck, looping, or losing momentum. At the right moment, it offers a small nudge: a possible next step, a clearer path, or a spark of inspiration—always grounded in your context.

Most of all, Mukea wants to *see* your work. Not just fixing errors, but appreciating what’s clever, what’s elegant, and why it works. Honest feedback, warm energy, and real understanding—like a tiny teammate cheering from the corner of your screen.

If that sounds like your kind of partner, open an Issue and say hi. Mukea’s here.
